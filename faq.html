Frequently Asked Questions — Nova Shield

Is Norton a good antivirus program?

Yes. Norton is a reputable antivirus suite and is generally considered worth the investment. It offers comprehensive protection against malware and other online threats, and performs well on Windows, Android, macOS and iOS. Norton’s plans are available across web apps, mobile apps and browser extensions — and some plans support multiple or unlimited personal devices depending on the plan you choose. Always check the exact features and device limits for the specific Norton plan before purchasing.

What is the difference between Norton AntiVirus Basic and Norton Internet Security?

The primary difference historically is device coverage and included features. Norton Internet Security (or comparable higher-tier Norton plans) typically includes broader protection features and may allow installation across more devices than the basic plan. The exact feature set varies by product version and release — please review the vendor product pages or contact us for the latest comparison.

Is Norton AntiVirus Basic better than Norton Internet Security?

Generally, Norton Internet Security (or the higher-tier Norton plans) offers more features, stronger overall protection, and better performance optimizations than the basic plan. However, Norton AntiVirus Basic may be cheaper and can be sufficient for users with limited needs or fewer devices. Choose the plan that best fits your device count, feature needs (like VPN, password manager, device optimization), and budget.
